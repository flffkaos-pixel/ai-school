About

Everything you need to know about this guide and how to contribute

What is awesome-generative-ai-guide?

Generative AI is experiencing rapid growth, and this repository serves as a comprehensive hub for updates on generative AI research, interview materials, notebooks, and more. Created and maintained by Aishwarya Naresh Reganti, this guide is updated regularly with the latest additions in the GenAI space.

What You'll Find Here

  • Monthly Best GenAI Papers List — Curated paper lists from 2024 through 2025
  • GenAI Interview Resources — 60 common interview questions with detailed answers
  • Original Courses — Applied LLMs Mastery, Agentic AI Crash Course, AI Evals, OpenClaw Mastery, and more
  • 90+ Free GenAI Courses — Curated from top universities, companies, and platforms
  • Practical Roadmaps — 3-day RAG, 5-day LLM foundations, 5-day AI agents
  • In-Depth Guides — Agents 101, fine-tuning 101, multimodal LLMs, and more
  • Code Notebooks — Links to hands-on repositories for building GenAI applications
  • Research Tables — Comprehensive categorized summaries of RAG, agentic search, and evaluation research
